</li>

</li>

</li>



<li><strong>Configure Menu:</strong>

<li>Go to <code>Appearance &gt; Menus</code> to create and customize your site&#8217;s menu.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading">Step 7: Customize Your Site</h4>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Customizer:</strong>

</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Widgets:</strong>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Utilize widgets to add functionality to your sidebar, footer, or other widget areas.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading">Step 8: Create Content</h4>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Add Posts and Pages:</strong>

<li>Start adding content to your site by making posts and pages.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Media Library:</strong>
